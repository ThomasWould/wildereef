<h2>Our Story</h2>		
		<p>Can you catch this wave? Each batch is made from all natural, organic and free trade teas with that Wilde flavor you love. </p> 		
			<h3>Who are We</h3>		
		<p>Wildereef started in 2018 by Derek Skiewert with a hope to bring a better brew of Kombucha. On a trip to Colorado Derek discovered a passion for not only Kombucha but sharing that same joy of small handcrafted batches to you his friends. Join the family, become a booch brother!</p>

			<h3>Our Values</h3>		
		<p>When starting Wildereef we wanted to be a part of something we could not only be proud of but pursue everyday. That is why Wildereef only brews small carefully crafted batches of Kombucha that are not just good for you but good for the local economy and global market as a whole as we only use fair trade organic ingredients. We cant wait for you to experience  what a pursuit of perfection tastes like!</p>		
			<h3>What Next</h3>		
		<p>Looking to the future we want to be able to continue to to brew the booch that we are proud to share with you all but continue to bring it to more and more people. </p>
